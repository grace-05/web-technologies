Time Trek Help

Instructions:

  • Your goal is to shoot as many targets as you can within 1 minute.
  • Click on targets to earn 10 points each.
  • If you hit a bullseye you'll get 50 points!
  • Keep an eye on your score and time throughout the game!

    • Controls:

  • Click on the targets to shoot

    • Scoring:

  • Hitting a target: +10 points
  • Hitting a bullseye: +50 points
  • Missing a shot: 0 points

    • Game over:

  • The level will finish after 60 seconds.
  • Once all four levels have finished, you will be redirected to the summary page.
  • Scores and stats will be saved and added up.
